The Comfort of Curves

The oasis of one's bedroom should be a space in which people can feel at ease and have a sense of relaxation. The Comfort of Curves is a collection that embraces the beauty of rounded forms and soft lines.

Each piece will embody the sense of groundedness that makes your space feel whole. With this collection, it will combine all things comfortable with the idea of creating a more fluid and organic environment.

Madeline Poster Bed

This simple and elegant poster bed can be both a canopy and a traditional bed frame. Made from solid ash and copper accent details, it can be effortlessly made into both styles with its easy and simplistic construction. This bed is the perfect two in one that can be smoothly interchangeable in a bedroom setting to be the center of attention.

Arc Bench

This light and airy bench is a perfect complement to the Madeline Poster bed. It is made up of a fully upholstered frame with a top cushion for comfort.

Full-Bodied Floor Lamp

A fun and energetic unique floor lamp. It is made up of a solid piece of ash wood with glass orbs of light. This piece is very lively and adds a sense of movement to a space.

Plump Nightstand

Although its main purpose is a nightstand, the Plum Nightstand can also be used as a stool. Its simplistic upholstered round form is complimented by an ash slab on top.

Embrace Vanity

Marcie Armoire
